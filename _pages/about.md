Looking for Students
======
I am currently looking for highly motivated postgraduate students to work on research projects in our group. Students with strong interests in data security, privacy protection, AI security, and large model security are especially welcome.

Candidates are expected to have solid foundations in mathematics, programming, and machine learning, as well as strong motivation for academic research and innovation. Students with backgrounds in computer science, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, data science, or related areas are encouraged to contact me by email.

If you are interested in joining our group, please send me your CV, transcript, and any supporting materials that can reflect your academic or research ability.

欢迎对数据安全、隐私保护、人工智能安全、大模型安全等方向有浓厚兴趣的同学加入课题组攻读硕士研究生。

希望学生具有较扎实的数学与编程基础，对科研有持续兴趣，具备良好的自主学习能力、沟通表达能力和一定的问题分析能力。具有网络空间安全、计算机科学与技术、人工智能、数据科学等相关专业背景的同学优先考虑。

有意报考或加入课题组的同学，请通过电子邮件联系，并附上个人简历、成绩单及能体现科研潜力的相关材料。
